Description
RainReady is designed to provide users with real-time updates on the weather conditions in their current location. Upon launching the app, the user is presented with a clean and intuitive interface that displays the current temperature, and other essential weather information such as wind speed. The app uses GPS to determine the user's location, ensuring that the weather information is accurate. One of its features is its ability to alert users when it starts raining. The app is equipped with a vibration feature that can vibrate when the chance of rain exceeds a certain threshold. This feature can be beneficial for people who want to stay dry.

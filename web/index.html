Description
This weather app is designed to provide users with real-time updates on the weather conditions in their current location. Upon launching the app, the user is presented with a clean and intuitive interface that displays the current temperature, as well as other key weather information like humidity, wind speed, and precipitation. The app uses GPS technology to determine the user's location, ensuring that the weather information is accurate and relevant.

One of the standout features of this weather app is its ability to alert users when it starts raining. The app is equipped with a vibration feature that can be customized to vibrate when the chance of rain exceeds a certain threshold. This feature can be incredibly helpful for people who want to stay dry and avoid getting caught in a sudden rainstorm.

In addition to the real-time weather updates, the app also provides hourly and daily forecasts, as well as radar maps and other weather data. Users can customize the app to display their preferred units of measurement, and they can also save multiple locations for easy access. Overall, this weather app is an excellent tool for anyone who wants to stay informed about the weather and be prepared for any conditions.
